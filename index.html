Meet The Designer

Hi, I'm Carla

I'm the Co-Owner and Designer behind Divine Creations, where every piece is handmade with care and a personal touch — from fresh floral arrangements to wooden decor, painted blocks, and clay pot characters.

Outside of Divine Creations, I serve as Church Administrator at Whiteash Free Will Baptist Church, and I'm a proud alum of John A. Logan College. Faith, family, and creativity guide everything I make.
